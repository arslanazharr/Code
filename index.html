Lets discuss the types of Digital Marketing:

Social Media Marketing (SMM):

Social Media Marketing is done to promote your business on social media platforms.

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO):

SEO is done to rank your website organically on SERP without running ads.

Search Engine Marketing (SEM):

When you run an ad to promote your webiste and rank on SERP.

Affliate Marketing:

You promote someone else's business online and get commission on every action.
